Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 13, 2026

1. Intimacy and Privacy by Design

Lovail is designed as a private sanctuary for couples. We believe your intimate moments should stay intimate. We do not sell your personal data, and we do not use it for advertising.

2. Data We Collect & Protect

  • Account Information: Name, email, and gender to personalize your experience and sync with your partner.
  • The Sanctuary Content: Photos, memories, and messages shared between you and your partner. These are stored with end-to-end encryption to ensure 100% privacy.
  • Love Pulse (Biometrics): Heart rate data used for real-time mood sharing. This data is processed locally and never stored on our servers in a permanent format unless used for specific health history features you opt into.
  • The Matcher: Your private preferences for Date Nights and intimacy are stored securely and only revealed when both partners match.

3. Technology & Security

We use industry-leading services to ensure your digital sanctuary remains impregnable:

  • Infrastructure: Secure authentication and real-time syncing via Firebase.
  • Encrypted Storage: All media is stored securely using advanced encryption protocols.
  • Biometric Lock: The app is secured via FaceID/Fingerprint locally on your device.

4. Our Commitment

Lovail is built on the foundation of trust. We never sell your data to third parties, we do not track your location for advertising, and we provide tools for you to wipe your shared sanctuary instantly if you ever choose to do so.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about your privacy, contact our team at contact@lovail.com.
